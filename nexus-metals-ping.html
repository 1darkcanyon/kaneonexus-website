// FIELD TIPS
SURFACE MATTERSStrike on bare rock or wood. Metal surfaces muddy the resonance with their own frequency.
STRIKE CLEANLYOne sharp tap is better than a dragged scrape. A clean tap produces a cleaner ring.
ALLOYS BLEND14k gold will read between gold and copper/silver. The blend shifts the dominant peak.
SILENCE HELPSBackground noise competes with the ring. Quiet field, still air = cleaner analysis.
SIZE AFFECTS PITCHLarger specimens ring lower; smaller ring higher. Compare to reference tones at multiple octaves.
USE BOTH PANELSListen to the reference tone first, then analyze. Your ear is a powerful instrument too.
