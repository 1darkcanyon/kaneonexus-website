NEXUS RESPONSE
🏠
ASSESSMENT
Your property is assigned an assessed value by the county assessor, which forms the tax base.
📋
LEVY RATE
Local governments set millage rates. Tax = Assessed Value × Rate. Rates vary by county and school district.
⚖️
APPEAL RIGHTS
You have the right to challenge your assessment. Most counties allow appeals within 30–90 days of notice.
🛡️
EXEMPTIONS
Homestead, senior, veteran, and disability exemptions can significantly reduce your taxable value.
